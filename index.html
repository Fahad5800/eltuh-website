AI & Drone Technology for Fire Safety

ELTUH is committed to transforming the way we prevent and respond to wildfires. Using advanced drone technology combined with powerful artificial intelligence, our systems can detect fire risks in real-time, monitor hazardous zones with unmatched precision, and deliver critical data to first responders when every second counts. Creating a safer, more sustainable future by protecting both people and nature is the main principal that governs our team. From early detection to rapid response, ELTUH is building innovative tools that empower communities, save lives, and reduce the devastating impact of wildfires across the globe.

About Us

ELTUH is a cutting-edge startup dedicated to leveraging AI and drone technology to combat wildfires. Our mission is to provide innovative solutions that enhance fire detection, monitoring, and response capabilities.

Our Vision in Action

We’re redefining fire safety through innovation and creativity. Here’s how:

Smart Detection

IR and thermal cameras with IoT sensors spot fire risks early. 360° sprayers, hydraulic towers, and hydro-solar panels enable fast, sustainable response.

Data Intelligence

AI risk assessment, real-time alerts, and control APIs give firefighters instant insights. FAA-approved auto-flights and tower charging ensure nonstop coverage.

Future Innovation

Scalable firetech with real-time monitoring, customer integration, and evacuation support—protecting forests, cities, and industries worldwide.
